# Research SOP

For full competitor profiles, see `context/competitive-landscape.md`.
For trusted sources, see `context/sources.md`.
For executing a research task, use `skills/market-research.md`.

---

## Market Research Process

1. **Define the question** — Market sizing, trend analysis, ICP validation, or industry intelligence
2. **Identify sources** — Use `context/sources.md`. Prioritise primary sources (SEC filings, official reports, company announcements)
3. **Cross-reference** — Verify any statistic against at least 2 independent sources before using
4. **Flag unverifiable claims** — Remove or hold for Amneh to confirm before publishing
5. **Summarise** — What → So What → Now What
6. **Document** — Update relevant context files if findings change strategy or messaging

---

## Competitor Monitoring Process

1. Review `context/competitive-landscape.md` quarterly
2. Check each competitor's LinkedIn, website, and news for: new products, pricing changes, messaging shifts, funding rounds, partnerships
3. Update competitor profiles with new information + date
4. Extract new content angles from weaknesses or shifts — add to relevant skill files
5. Flag any move into RealRate's certification territory (seal/badge products) immediately

---

## Content Research Process (for posts + articles)

1. Always check realrate-archive.com first for ECR data
2. For external claims: use sources from `context/sources.md`
3. For company-specific facts: SEC EDGAR or company's investor relations page
4. For industry stats: IBISWorld, Statista, Grand View Research
5. For macro context: Reuters, CNBC, FT, WSJ
6. Never include a statistic that cannot be traced to a primary source
