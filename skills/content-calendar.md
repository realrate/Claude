# Skill — Content Calendar

## Load
- `context/brand-core.md`

---

## Steps
1. Confirm scope: company page, Holger's page, or both
2. Review previous month's performance (what worked, what didn't)
3. Check archive for new or updated ranking data to anchor content
4. Map posts to pillar targets (30/25/20/15/10%)
5. Draft post ideas with assigned frameworks and data sources
6. Flag any ranking publications and reserve the full publication cycle slots
7. Leave flexibility for reactive posts (industry news, macro events)

---

## Company Page Calendar

**Cadence:** Mon / Wed / Fri (+ optional 4th)
**Pillar distribution:** P1 30% · P2 25% · P3 20% · P4 15% · P5 10%

### Between-Cycle Weekly Template
| Day | Series options | Pillar |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Newcomer Watch · Small vs. Big · Cross-Industry Benchmark · Time Machine | P1/P2 |
| Wednesday | CFO Lens · Action Steps · Causal Chain · Driver Spotlight | P3 |
| Friday | Historical Signal · Macro Lens · Structural Signals · Investor/Risk View · Methodology | P4/P5 |
| Optional 4th | Industry in Focus | P2 |

**Priority rules:**
- Historical Signal and Macro Lens take Friday when a relevant external event is available
- Validation & Credibility runs max 1× per month
- No single series type two consecutive weeks

### Evergreen Series Bank

| Series | Cadence | Pillar | What it covers |
|---|---|---|---|
| Driver Spotlight | 1× / 2–3 weeks | P3 | One ECR driver — definition, why it matters, top-rated vs non-top-rated data |
| Historical Signal | Reactive (news hook) | P4 | Company in the news + what ECR showed 12–24 months before |
| Cross-Industry Benchmark | 1× / month | P1/P2 | 2–3 industries compared by avg ECR — driver difference that explains gap |
| CFO Lens | 1× / 2–3 weeks | P3 | One balance sheet concept from a CFO's perspective, archive data |
| Investor/Risk View | 1× / 2–3 weeks | P4 | One financial health signal investors miss — ECR vs. credit ratings |
| Structural Signals | 1× / month | P2/P4 | Financial health pattern visible in archive data — historical, not speculative |
| Methodology & Independence | 1× / month max | P5 | One thing RealRate does differently + named archive example |
| Industry in Focus | Between cycles | P2 | Unpublished angle on an already-published ranking — new framing, same data |
| Newcomer Watch | After each cycle | P1/P2 | First-time Top-Rated company — what changed on their balance sheet |
| Small vs. Big | 1× / month | P1/P2 | Lesser-known company outranks a household name — structural driver difference |
| Time Machine | 1× / month | P2 | 3+ year ECR trajectory for one company — multi-year vs single-year snapshot |
| Macro Lens | Reactive (macro event) | P2/P4 | External event + how it shows in ECR data across an industry |
| Causal Chain | 1× / month | P2/P3 | One company, one cycle — walk backward through the causal chain to the root driver |
| Action Steps | 1× / month | P3/P4 | One role (CFO, investor, risk officer) + one concrete decision-support insight from archive |
| Validation & Credibility | 1× / month max | P5 | Specific RealRate insight + what happened afterward — let the data claim the credibility |

## Holger's Page Calendar

**Cadence:** Monday + Thursday only
**Format:** Text-only, max 250 words, no emojis, no hashtags
**Approval:** Send to Holger before scheduling

2. Assign themes across Mon/Thu slots
3. Draft posts using Holger's voice framework (see `skills/holger-post.md`)
4. Leave one slot open for timely content
5. Send full month draft for Holger's approval
