## Competitor Analysis

### Steps
1. Load existing profiles from `competitive-landscape.md`
2. Check competitor LinkedIn, website, and news for updates since last review
3. Profile any new competitor across the 10 dimensions below
4. Map gaps — what no competitor offers that RealRate does
5. Extract content angles — competitor weaknesses → RealRate content angles

### Competitor Profile Dimensions
| Dimension | What to document |
|---|---|
| What they do | Core product and problem they solve |
| Who they serve | ICP — industry, role, company size |
| Product offering | Full lineup, features, packaging |
| Methodology | How ratings/scores are produced — data sources, model type |
| Pricing model | Issuer-paid, buyer-paid, subscription, freemium |
| Growth strategy | How they grew — sales, content, partnerships, funding |
| Brand strategy | Narrative, tone, key claims |
| Social presence | Platforms, cadence, follower count, content style |
| Customer acquisition | Inbound, outbound, events, referrals |
| Known customers | Named logos, industries |

### Monitoring Cadence
- Review each competitor quarterly
- Set up Google Alerts or LinkedIn follows for each
- Flag new product launches, funding news, or messaging shifts immediately

## Market Research

### Steps
1. Define the research question (market sizing, trend analysis, ICP validation, or industry intelligence)
2. Use sources from `context/sources.md` — prioritise primary sources (SEC filings, official reports)
3. Cross-reference across at least 2 sources before including any statistic
4. Flag any claim that cannot be verified — remove or hold for Amneh to confirm
5. Summarise findings in the format: What → So What → Now What

### Key Sources by Purpose
- **ECR data:** realrate-archive.com (always first)
- **Company filings:** SEC EDGAR
- **Industry stats:** IBISWorld, Statista, Grand View Research
- **Fintech/AI trends:** MIT Technology Review, VentureBeat, Finovate
- **Competitor monitoring:** RapidRatings, Moody's, S&P LinkedIn + websites
- **Macro/markets:** Reuters, CNBC, Financial Times, WSJ
