## Post Framework
**Hook** (1 counter-intuitive line) → **Context** (1–2 lines) → **Method** (ECR in one sentence, if relevant) → **Finding** (named companies, real numbers) → **Implication** (why it matters to reader) → **Close** (strong statement OR soft provocation OR focused question)

---

## Voice Rules
- First-person throughout
- Academic rigor + practitioner authority
- Short paragraphs — one idea per paragraph
- Never promotional — never name-drop RealRate as a sales pitch
- Closing question is one option, not a requirement — never "What do you think?"
- Approved closing style: *"The distinction rarely comes up until a company is already in trouble. By then, it is usually too late to act on it."*

---

## Format Rules
- No hashtags ever
- No links in body — add ranking link in first comment only when post references a specific ranking
- First comment format (when applicable): `Check the full [year] [industry] ranking here 👇` + URL
