## Instantly Email Campaign

### Steps
1. Define ICP for the campaign (which of the 6 ICPs, which industry)
2. Build or source lead list — verified emails only
3. Write sequence (structure below)
4. Set up A/B variants — minimum 2 subject line variants
5. Launch to 100 leads first — monitor 5–7 days before scaling
6. Adjust based on results — never scale underperforming campaigns

### Email Sequence Structure (5 emails)
| Email | Timing | Goal | Tone |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email 1 | Day 1 | Open + first impression | Peer-to-peer, data-led hook |
| Email 2 | Day 3–4 | Build interest | Different angle — insight or pain point |
| Email 3 | Day 7–8 | Social proof | Bayerische testimonial or industry finding |
| Email 4 | Day 12–14 | Soft CTA | Low-pressure call offer |
| Email 5 | Day 20–22 | Final touch | Keep door open, no pressure |

### Per Email Structure
- **Subject:** Short, specific, curiosity-driven — no clickbait
- **Opening:** Personalised observation — their industry, role, or a relevant data point
- **Body:** Pain point → RealRate value prop → one specific, verifiable claim
- **CTA:** One clear action — never more than one ask per email

### ICP Messaging Anchors
| ICP | Lead With | Value Proposition |
|---|---|---|
| Top-Rated Companies | Financial transparency as a trust signal | Seal + free evaluation + benchmarking |
| Non Top-Rated | Gap between perceived and actual financial health | Consulting + clear improvement path |
| SME / Private Equity | Lack of credible third-party validation | Seal + report + workshop |
| Investors | Unreliable or conflicted rating sources | Independent database + AI reports |
| Supply Chain | Hidden financial risk in supplier base | Early warning — flag risk 12 months out |

### Target Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Bounce Rate | ~5%, max 10% |
| Open Rate | 40–60% |
| Reply Rate | ~6% |
| Interested Rate | ~1% |
| Call Booked Rate | 0.66% |

## LinkedIn DM Sequence

### Steps
1. Identify trigger: prospect commented, reacted, or visited profile
2. Send connection request with personalised note (1–2 lines, no pitch)
3. Follow DM sequence below
4. Qualify before suggesting a call

### Connection Request Template
> *"[Name], came across your [post/profile/comment] on [topic] — [one specific observation]. Would love to connect with others thinking about [relevant theme]."*

### DM Sequence
| Touch | Timing | Approach |
|---|---|---|
| Message 1 | Day 1 post-connection | Insight + genuine question about their work |
| Message 2 | Day 4–5 (no reply) | Different angle — new data point or industry observation |
| Message 3 | Day 10–12 (no reply) | Soft close — offer something of value |
| Message 4 | Day 20+ | Final touch — keep door open |

### Move to a Call
Only after at least one genuine exchange:
> *"Happy to share the full [industry] data if useful — we could do a quick 20-minute walk-through if that's easier than back-and-forth here."*
