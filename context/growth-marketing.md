## LinkedIn Strategy — Company Page (Primary Focus)

> The Company Page is the **primary focus**. Holger's personal page is secondary — only address it if explicitly requested.

### Page Stats
- ~5,000 followers
- Transition goal: shift from broad engagement → precision targeting (CFOs, investors, risk officers, actuaries)

### Posting Cadence
- **3–4× per week:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday (+ optional 4th post)
- **Monday:** Data reveal / ranking highlight (P1)
- **Wednesday:** Insight / analysis / driver education (P2 or P3) — preferred day for major ranking announcements
- **Friday:** Risk signal / methodology / trust / BOFU (P4 or P5)
- **Optional 4th:** Industry news reaction post (timely content)
- **Holger's personal page:** Monday + Thursday only

### Caption Formula
**Context → Insight → Implication**
- Context: What's the situation or data point?
- Insight: What does RealRate's analysis reveal?
- Implication: Why does this matter to the reader's role?

### Format Rules
- Max 2 emojis in caption, none on image
- 3–5 hashtags only (niche over broad)
- Always link to archive or methodology — never sales pages or pricing
- Tag companies in first pinned comment, not in caption (max 3–5 in caption if essential)
- Every analytical post must include tagline: `"Powered by RealRate: Using Explainable Financial AI"`

## Content Pillars — Company Page

### P1 — Ranking & Recognition (30% · TOFU)
**Goal:** Reach. Named companies, real ECR numbers, industry benchmarks.
- Top 10 rankings by industry
- New entrants & dropouts
- ECR benchmark vs. industry average
- Top-Rated seal announcements
- Year-over-year rank movements

### P2 — Insight & Interpretation (25% · MOFU)
**Goal:** Credibility. Go deeper on ranking results. Explain what moved and why.
- Why companies moved up/down
- Structural changes driving ECR shifts
- Chart interpretation posts
- Industry comparison analysis

### P3 — Drivers of Financial Stability (20% · MOFU)
**Goal:** Education + authority. What actually drives financial health.
- Capital buffers & equity strength
- Debt structure & liabilities
- Cash-flow volatility signals
- Revenue vs. financial strength

### P4 — Risk & Warning Signals (15% · MOFU→BOFU)
**Goal:** High-stakes relevance for risk officers, investors, procurement.
- ECR decline patterns before distress
- Hidden balance-sheet risks
- Industry fragility comparisons
- Insolvency leading indicators

### P5 — Methodology & Trust (10% · BOFU)
**Goal:** Conversion. Independence, transparency, what RealRate refuses to do.
- ECR methodology explained
- What RealRate does NOT do
- Independence as a principle
- Testimonial posts (Bayerische)

## 5-Phase Ranking Publication Protocol

| Phase | Timing | Post Type | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 5–3 days before | Teaser only. No data. Name the industry. | Build anticipation |
| 2 | Day 0 (Wednesday preferred) | Full ranking, top 10, ECR numbers, archive link in pinned comment | Maximum reach. Pin this post. |
| 3 | Day +1 or +2 | First insight post. Most surprising finding. Named company. | Depth layer |
| 4 | Day +3 to +5 | Structural or risk perspective. Fragility patterns or driver deep-dive. | Credibility layer |
| 5 | Week 2+ (optional) | Respond to comments, post reactions, share data from DMs | Conversion layer |
