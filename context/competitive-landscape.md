# RealRate — Competitive Landscape

## Competitors

**RapidRatings** *(primary competitor)*
- Financial health rating platform for supply chain and credit risk
- Uses quantitative analysis of financial statements — score-based output
- Sold to buyers/investors, not the rated companies
- No causal AI — correlation-based, no driver explanation
- No public archive — data is paywalled
- No certification/seal product

**Moody's / S&P** *(traditional incumbent)*
- Nationally Recognized Statistical Rating Organizations (NRSROs)
- Issuer-paid model — companies pay to be rated, creating conflict of interest
- Subjective analyst judgment + quantitative models — not fully explainable
- Slow, expensive, inaccessible to smaller companies
- RealRate was founded specifically as a response to this model

**CausalLens** *(causal AI technology competitor)*
- AI company focused on causal machine learning — not financial ratings specifically
- Competes on the causal AI methodology layer, not the financial intelligence product
- No ratings, no seal, no public archive
- Relevant as a comparison when positioning RealRate's AI approach
